FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI agent?

An AI agent is an autonomous software system that uses large language models to perceive its environment, make decisions, and take actions on your behalf — managing email, scheduling meetings, posting on social media, writing code, conducting research, and more — without you needing to micromanage every step.

How long does setup take?

Cloud: 24-48 hours from purchase to live agent. Local: 5-7 days including hardware configuration (if applicable), software deployment, and security audit. Enterprise setups with custom skills may take 10-14 days.

Cloud or local — which should I choose?

Choose Cloud if you want to start fast with lower upfront costs and the ability to scale on demand. Choose Local if you're planning to use AI for 2+ years and want zero monthly costs after setup. Local pays for itself in 6-12 months compared to cloud. Book a call and we'll help you decide.

What AI models can I use?

Cloud: GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, Grok, Llama, Mistral, DeepSeek via OpenRouter. Bring your own API key for any provider, or let us recommend the best option for your use case and budget. Self-hosted: Any Ollama-compatible model (Qwen, Llama, Hermes, Mistral) for $0 ongoing cost.

How secure is it?

Every deployment — cloud or local — uses a secure sandbox environment with Docker isolation, encrypted credentials, and firewall rules. Cloud deployments run on isolated VPS instances. Local deployments keep all data on your network — nothing leaves your server.

What if I need help after setup?

Every setup includes a support period. After that, Managed Care plans provide ongoing monitoring, updates, and priority support to keep your agents running smoothly. Available for both cloud and local setups.

Can I switch from Cloud to Local later?

Yes. We offer an upgrade path from cloud to local hosting. Your agent configuration, skills, and memory are migrated to your local hardware. Contact us for a migration quote based on your current setup.

Do you offer refunds?

If we can't get your agent running within the promised timeline, you get a full refund. No questions asked. We stand behind our work 100%.
