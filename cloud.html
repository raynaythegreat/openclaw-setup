FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I sign up?
We book a discovery call to discuss your needs, preferred integrations, and any workflow automations you want. From there, we deploy your agent within 48 hours and walk you through the dashboard.
Can I upgrade or downgrade later?
Yes. You can switch tiers at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. All your agent configuration, workflows, and memory are preserved.
What's the uptime guarantee?
Every cloud plan includes a 100% uptime SLA backed by continuous monitoring and automatic restart on failure. In the unlikely event of extended downtime, we'll apply a credit to your account.
Do I need my own API keys?
You can bring your own API keys from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, xAI, or any other provider. If you'd prefer a recommendation based on your use case and budget, we'll point you in the right direction during the onboarding call.
What AI models are supported?
GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, Grok, and self-hosted open-source options (Llama, Mistral, Qwen via Ollama). We configure the right model for each workflow during setup.
Is my data secure?
All cloud deployments use Docker sandboxing for full process isolation, AES-256 encrypted credential storage, OAuth2 where available, and strict firewall rules. Each tenant runs on its own isolated instance — your data never co-mingles with other customers.
