FAQ

Common Questions

What hardware do I need?
Mac Mini M2/M4 with 64GB RAM is our top recommendation — it's quiet, energy-efficient, and powerful enough for most workloads. We can also work with Linux servers or hardware you already own. We'll assess your use case and recommend accordingly.
How does self-hosted AI work?
We install Ollama with models like Qwen, Llama, and Mistral that run entirely on your hardware. No API keys, no usage bills, no data leaving your machine. The models download once and run locally from that point forward.
What if something breaks?
Every installation includes 30 days of post-setup support — email and async help to get any issues resolved. For ongoing peace of mind, optional monthly support plans are available so you always have someone to call.
Can I add cloud AI later?
Yes. Nemoclaw supports hybrid mode — use local models for the bulk of tasks (free) and route complex reasoning to cloud AI like Claude or GPT when needed. You control the routing rules.
How long does setup take?
Typically 2–4 hours for the remote installation session, including Ollama setup, model downloads, skills configuration, and integration testing. After the session your agent is live and ready to use.
Is it really $0/month?
Yes. After the one-time setup fee, there are zero recurring charges from us. Your AI runs on your hardware using Ollama's open-source models. You pay your own electricity bill and, optionally, a support plan — nothing else.
